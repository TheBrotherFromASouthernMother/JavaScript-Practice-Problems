Los Andes

El Sabor del Perú en la corazón de Texas

Welcome to Los Andes Peruvian Grill! We are family owned and operated and have brought the best of Andean cuisine to the Greater Houston area since 1977. Select from any of our delicious entrees cooked to perfection by our award winning chefs.

Lomo Saltado $22

Chifa influenced wok sautéed angus sirloin strips, tomatoes, onions, kiko soy, beef stock, red wine vinegar and cilantro, served with steak fries and white rice.

Chicharron $13

Confit pork spare ribs, with essence of cinnamon, bay leaf and lime, fried and served with mote, fried potato and topped with a queso blanco, tomato, and onion salsa

Lomo Montado $17

Pan seared top sirloin steak, deglazed with marsalla wine, served with roasted potatoes, white rice, topped with fried eggs and chef salad.

Pollos A Los Andes $15

Strips of chicken breast, sautéed with chorizo, cherry and banana peppers, onions, tomatoes, finished in a garlic-wine broth, served on a bed of steak fries topped with mozzarella cheese and scallions.

El Vegetariano $16

Medley of house grilled vegetables served with your choice of veggie Chaufa rice or arroz con queso.

Bistec A Lo Pobre $23

Pan seared top sirloin, in a jus, served with sweet plantains, french fries, white rice and topped with fried eggs.
